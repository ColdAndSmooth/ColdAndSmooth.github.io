My Story

As a recent CompSci graduate from an incredible university, my passion for music now combined with knowledge of technology has developed into an interest in production and showmanship. I still work as a software developer, which has created a high value for professionalism and integrity in my every day life - so expect a practiced professional.

The Music

Having just come from the college party scene, I keep up to date on all newly released Trap, HipHop, Top40 Pop, and many other genres widely played at bars, clubs and house parties. Not only is the music key, but the way songs are blended together to form a continuous, flowing set is immensely important. Check out my mixes, follow me, and let's party.
